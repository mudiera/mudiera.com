Who is Mudiera?

Mudiera is a digital ecosystem connecting Muslim women to businesses, services, and opportunities that align with their values.

Our mission

To make it effortless for Muslim women to discover trusted, halal-aligned businesses and experiences, while spotlighting the women who build, serve, and lead those spaces.

Mudiera curates, organises, and presents information in a way that saves time, reduces overwhelm, and centres Muslim women.

What we do

  • • Curate a directory of Muslim women owned and led businesses.
  • • Highlight events, workshops, and gatherings relevant to the community.
  • • Create space for collaboration, networking, and storytelling.
  • • Help brands reach Muslim women with respect and cultural understanding.
